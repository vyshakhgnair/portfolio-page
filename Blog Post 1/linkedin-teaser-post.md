## 📌 HOW TO POST THIS

### Step 1 — Post the content above (without the link)
Paste everything from "I failed JEE..." down to the hashtags directly into LinkedIn. **Do NOT include the blog link in the post body.**

### Step 2 — Add the blog link as the FIRST comment
Immediately after posting, go to your own post and leave the first comment:

> 📖 Full story here → [your blog URL]
> *Every chapter — the dual degree grind, the startup journey, the placement wall, and what finally changed.*

This is critical. LinkedIn's algorithm reduces reach for posts with external links in the body. First comment gets around this.

### Step 3 — Attach your graduation photo
Upload your **IIT Madras graduation photo** (the one with the IITM stole and degree certificate) as the image on this post. A real photo stops the scroll far better than text alone.

### Step 4 — Tag 2–3 people
Tag a friend, mentor, or batchmate who was part of the journey. Their engagement in the first hour will significantly boost reach.

### Step 5 — Engage fast
Reply to every comment within the first 60 minutes. LinkedIn's algorithm ranks posts heavily on early engagement. Even a one-line reply counts.

---

## ⏰ BEST TIME TO POST
**Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday — between 6:00 PM and 8:00 PM IST**
Avoid Mondays (people catching up) and Fridays (people mentally checked out).
