## VERSION A — Story Hook (Recommended for max reach)

---

When I was 16, my father asked me:
**"Do you want to become a CA like me?"**

I said no.

That one word changed the next 7 years of my life. 👇

---

I didn't have a clear plan.
I just knew I was drawn to **Computer Science, Mathematics, and Physics.**

My dad gave me one piece of advice:
*"If you're choosing CS — find a field with a future."*

So I researched.

That's when I found **Artificial Intelligence** — around 2017.
The paper *"Attention Is All You Need"* had just come out.

Did I understand it? **Not even a little.** 😄

But I had a strong gut feeling:
👉 *This is the future.*

So I chose it. Before I understood it.

---

**What followed was 7 years of:**

→ Choosing CS stream & learning Python
→ Not qualifying JEE — but getting into Kerala Engineering Entrance ✅
→ Enrolling in **IIT Madras BSc Data Science** alongside my BTech
→ Balancing two degrees, self-study, missed events, sleepless nights
→ Coming back to Andrew Ng's ML course — and going DEEP this time
→ Winning hackathons
→ Co-founding a startup → scaled to **₹5 crore valuation**
→ Becoming **Founding Engineer at Corr Robotics**
→ Publishing research (IEEE + journal contributions)
→ Facing brutal **placement rejections** despite all of the above
→ Analyzing, improving, persisting
→ Finally — **multiple offers**
→ Joining **Rappit as an AI/ML Engineer** 🎯

---

**The hardest part wasn't the dual degrees.**
It wasn't the startup.
It wasn't even the rejections.

It was staying consistent when nothing seemed to be working.

---

If you're somewhere in the middle of your journey right now —
still grinding, still getting rejected, still unsure —

Here's what I want you to know:

**The gap between where you are and where you want to be
is closed by one thing: not stopping.**

## PRO TIPS FOR MAX REACH

- **Post time:** 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM IST on Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday (highest engagement windows)
- **Don't put the link in the post** — LinkedIn reduces reach for posts with external links. Put it in the **first comment**.
- **Reply to every comment fast** — LinkedIn's algorithm boosts posts with early engagement. Reply within 30–60 minutes of posting.
- **Tag 2–3 people** who were part of your journey (friends, mentors) — they'll likely engage, boosting visibility
- **Save the post** yourself — tells the algorithm the content is worth bookmarking
- **First 60 minutes matter most** — LinkedIn ranks content heavily based on early engagement
